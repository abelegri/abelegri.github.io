Alexa Belegrin

Engineer. Designer. Humanitarian.

Alexandra Belegrin is a undergraduate student in Purdue University's Multidisciplinary Engineering Program where she is currently obtaining a degree in General Engineering with minors in Electrical Engineering, Political Science, and LGBT studies. She has also been involved with Honors First Year Engineering as a Teaching Assistant for the past three years, and has helped restructure the course and create assignments. Alexandra's interests are in Human Centered Design Engineering, specifically the design of the built environment and usability testing. She is also involved with the entrepreneurship community on Purdue's campus and brings that spirit into all aspects of her life. When not focused on working, Alexandra enjoys visiting her family, caring for her plants, and cross stitching.
