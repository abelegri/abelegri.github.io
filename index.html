About Alexa

Alexa Belegrin is a undergraduate student in Purdue University's Engineering Education Program where she is currently obtaining a degree in General Engineering and working as a Peer Teaching Assistant for Honors First Year Engineering. She is a strong advocate for health, wellness, diversity, and inclusion in academia. She has been the president of Purdue's 3 Day Startup organization since spring, 2018. Alexa also enjoys caring for her extensive plant family, reading, and embroidery.

A jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one.
